# ACORD 28 - Evidence of Commercial Property Insurance

## Purpose
Provides detailed evidence of commercial property insurance to lenders, mortgagees, and loss payees. More comprehensive than ACORD 27 for commercial properties.

## Supported Coverage Types
- Commercial Building Coverage
- Business Personal Property (BPP)
- Business Income/Extra Expense
- Equipment Breakdown
- Tenant Improvements

## When to Use
- Commercial property mortgage requirements
- SBA loan documentation
- Commercial lease with property coverage requirements
- Complex commercial property coverage evidence

## Key Sections

### 1. Producer Information
Agency/broker details

### 2. Named Insured
Business entity that owns/leases the property

### 3. Insurance Company
Property insurance carrier with NAIC code

### 4. Property Location
Complete address of insured location

### 5. Coverage Information
- Building coverage amount
- BPP coverage amount
- Business income limit
- Valuation method
- Coinsurance percentage
- Deductibles
- Covered perils (Basic, Broad, Special)

### 6. Loss Payee/Mortgagee
- Name and address
- Loan/Account number
- Type of interest

### 7. Additional Coverages
- Flood insurance (if applicable)
- Earthquake coverage
- Equipment breakdown

## Important Rules
- More detailed than ACORD 27 for commercial properties
- Must accurately reflect policy terms
- Loss payee must be endorsed to policy
- Business income calculations should match policy
- Note any sublimits or coverage restrictions
